Deep Learning for Pattern Recognition in Complex Systems

Dr. Sarah Jenkins, Michael Chen, et al.

calendar_today Published: Oct 2023

Identifiers doi: 10.1038/s41586-023-0000-1 open_in_new
Keywords
Neural Networks Pattern Rec AI Models Systems
Abstract

This paper introduces a novel approach to identifying multi-dimensional patterns in complex systems utilizing deep convolutional architectures. By leveraging synthetic data augmentation alongside empirical datasets, the proposed model demonstrates a 15% improvement in anomaly detection accuracy. We discuss the implications for scalable infrastructure and real-time inference engines.
